Video Games

The Elder Scrolls

In a universe where dragons speak their own language, where lizard-men dwell, and where sorcery is prevalent in every corner of the world, the gamer is thrust into one of the greatest game series ever created. In the Elder Scrolls, humans, elves, and orcs all have cities and politics that rules them. Other non-human races, such as the Khajit and Argonians, display animal-like qualities that aid them in their daily lives. Organized religions, cultures, and other communities all shape the lore and the setting of Tamriel, the continent featured prominently in the games. Tamriel has nine unique regions, with a unique race living in each of them. The protagonist must learn skills to advance in the world, with a wide array of magic, books and other obecjtives to learn, read from and complete. Jeremy Soule, a critically acclaimed music composer, has scored the main Elder Scrolls games since 2002. With Soule's original score, featured on this page, open world immersion gameplay, and high-level digital graphics, The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim game, along with the franchise itself, has been lauded as the greatest video game(s) in recent years, revolutionizing game development, gameplay, and entertainment.

The Dark Souls Franchise

Created by Hidetaka Miyazaki and FromSoftware, the Souls franchise is one of the most critically acclaimed video game franchises of all time. The player is able to create a character from scratch, giving it whataver name, gender, and class desired. The classes are Knight, Barbarian, Thief, and Mage. Abilities showcased by the character may be modified according to in-game choices that have been made. The player is able to gain souls in order to equip their arsenal as well as receive increases in experience to raise their levels. The franchise is both lauded and slightly criticized for its difficulty, with Dark Souls III being famous for having incredibly complex fights. The games have spawned a comic book and a board game, as well. The franchise's music, written by Motoi Sakuraba, is also regarded as a spectacular work of art.

World of Warcraft (WoW)

The World of Azeroth beckons! In what is Blizzard Entertainment's most famous work, the Warcraft franchise has made MMORPGs (massively multiplayer online roleplaying games) a mainstream wonder. Massive tournaments are held worldwide, and novels, comics, board games, card games, manga and even a film all based on the game are enjoyed by millions. Azeroth is one of many planets featured prominently in the games. It features four continents which are called the Eastern Kingdoms. Players get to participate in journeying on quests, engaging in combat with mythical and fantastic monsters, and expanding their 'skill tree' to gain experience and become stronger. As more titles have been released, the Warcraft universe has expanded exponentially. The lore and the art becomes richer and the poularity of the game increases.
The Legend of Zelda
In what is arguably one of Nintendo's most famous game franchises, The Legend of Zelda focuses on an elf named Link who often has to save the Kingdom of Hyrule and Princess Zelda. He must also fight numerous enemies, epecially Ganon, a demon who is his main foe. Link has had many versions of his character over the years, and has been tasked with keeping the Triforce of Courage, one of three extremely powerful objects left in the world by three goddesses who personified three qualities: Power, Courage, and Wisdom. Princess Zelda is the keeper of the Triforce of Wisdom, while Ganon usually has the Triforce of Power. Ganon, being the villain he is, desires the two other Triforces for his personal gain. If someone manages to unite the three Triforces, the user becomes omnipotent. Since 1986, nineteen games have been released featuring the adventures of Zelda and Link, including an American television series and a plethora of Japnese manga series.
